# Design Brainstorming: Currency Converter

<text>
**Design Movement**: Neumorphism (Soft UI)
**Core Principles**:
- Soft, extruded shapes for inputs and cards.
- Minimalist color palette to reduce eye strain.
- High focus on tactile interaction (buttons feel "pressed").
**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: Off-white / Light Grey (#F0F2F5) to allow shadows to pop.
- Accents: Soft Blue (#4B7BEC) for primary actions, indicating trust and stability.
**Layout Paradigm**:
- Centralized "Card" floating in the middle of the screen.
- mimicking a physical calculator or tool.
**Signature Elements**:
- Soft inner/outer shadows for input fields.
- Rounded corners (20px+) for a friendly feel.
**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Inputs should feel like they are being pressed into the screen.
- Smooth transitions for number updates.
**Animation**:
- Gentle pulse on the exchange rate when it updates.
- Smooth number counting animation when converting.
**Typography System**:
- Headings: Nunito (Rounded sans-serif) for a friendly vibe.
- Body: Roboto or Open Sans for legibility.
</text>

- Classic "Search Result" card style.
- Clean, left-aligned text with a prominent converter box.
**Signature Elements**:
- The "Swap" button circle icon.
- Big, clear input fields with floating labels.
- A mini-chart visualization (static or simple SVG) to mimic the financial context.
**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Instant feedback.
- Ripple effects on buttons.
**Animation**:
- Standard Material ease-in-out transitions.
**Typography System**:
- Font: Google Sans (or similar geometric sans like Poppins) for headers.

- Split screen: Left side large typography (Rate), Right side functional inputs.
**Signature Elements**:
- Huge, bold exchange rate display.
- Minimalist lines and dividers.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Snappy, instant changes. No motion blur.
**Animation**:
- Sharp cuts.
**Typography System**:
- Helvetica Now (or Inter/Arial) - Bold, tight tracking.
